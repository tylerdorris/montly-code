The Rules

Trap rules

There are five positions in trap. five shooters on the line one is a squad leader. Once everyone is ready the sqaud leader will say pull. The trap house will shoot out a bird, the leader will shoot then the next will.

skeet rules

in skeet there are 8 positions and five shooters. there is a high house and a low house. the first shooter will yell pull and the high house will shoot out a bird, then the shooter will fire. then he says pull again and it will shoot from the low house then the shooter will fire. lastly the shooter will say pull one last time and the high and low house will shoot. after the shooter fires three times they will switch shooters after all shooters they will go to a different position.
